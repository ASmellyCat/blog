---
title: "Small Hours"
date: 2026-02-20
category: murmurs
description: "A brief thought on the quiet between moments."
---

There's a kind of silence that only exists at 3 AM. Not the absence of sound — the city still hums, the fridge still clicks — but the absence of expectation. No one is waiting for you to do anything. No one is watching.

In those small hours, you belong entirely to yourself.
