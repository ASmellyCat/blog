Made coffee. Stood by the window for a while, watching the street below. A woman walked her dog past the same lamppost three times. I wondered if the dog was leading or if she just liked that particular stretch of sidewalk.

Read half a chapter of something I'll probably never finish. Made a note to buy more oat milk. Forgot the note.
